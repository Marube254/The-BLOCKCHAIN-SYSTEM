Blockchain Verification

How Blockchain Secures Your Vote

The IUEA Blockchain Voting System uses SHA-256 cryptographic hashing to ensure that every vote is immutable, transparent, and verifiable.

SHA-256(voter_id + candidate_id + sector + timestamp + previous_hash)

Each vote is linked to the previous vote using its hash, creating an unbreakable chain. Any attempt to alter a vote would break the entire chain, making tampering immediately detectable.

Your Vote Receipt

After voting, you receive a unique transaction hash. You can use this hash to verify your vote was counted without revealing your identity.
