Terms of Service

1. Acceptance of Terms

By accessing and using the IUEA Blockchain Voting System, you agree to be bound by these Terms of Service. If you do not agree, please do not use the system.

2. Eligibility

You must be a registered student at the International University of East Africa (IUEA) with a valid admission number to vote. Each student is entitled to one vote only.

3. Voting Rules

  • Each voter can cast only one vote per election.
  • Votes are final and cannot be changed once submitted.
  • All votes are recorded on the blockchain and are immutable.
  • Fingerprint verification is optional but recommended for security.

4. Privacy & Data Protection

Your personal information is protected in accordance with our Privacy Policy. Fingerprint data is stored as cryptographic credentials, not actual fingerprint images.

5. Prohibited Conduct

  • Attempting to vote multiple times
  • Impersonating another student
  • Manipulating or tampering with vote data
  • Using automated scripts or bots

6. Account Security

You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your password. The system uses bcrypt hashing and Sanctum tokens for security.

IUEA reserves the right to modify these terms at any time. Continued use of the system constitutes acceptance of modified terms.
