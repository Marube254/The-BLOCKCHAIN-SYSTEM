Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register to vote?
Click on "Register" on the login page. Enter your first name, last name, admission number, email, and create a password. After registration, you will receive a confirmation email.
What is blockchain voting?
Blockchain voting uses SHA-256 cryptographic hashing to secure each vote. Each vote is linked to the previous vote, creating an immutable chain that prevents tampering or alteration.
Can I change my vote after submitting?
No. Once a vote is cast and recorded on the blockchain, it is immutable and cannot be changed, altered, or deleted.
Is my vote private?
Yes. Your vote is encrypted and only the blockchain hash is stored. Your identity is not directly linked to your vote choice.
What if I forget my password?
Click "Forgot Password" on the login page. Enter your registered email address, and a password reset link will be sent to you.
What browsers are supported?
The system works best on Google Chrome, Microsoft Edge, and Firefox. For fingerprint verification, Chrome or Edge is recommended.
How do I know my vote was counted?
After voting, you receive an email confirmation with a unique transaction hash. You can use this hash to verify your vote was counted.
