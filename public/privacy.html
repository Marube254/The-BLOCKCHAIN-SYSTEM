Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to the IUEA Blockchain Voting System. Your privacy is of utmost importance to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our voting system. Please read this policy carefully before registering and voting.

By using the IUEA Blockchain Voting System, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

When you register to vote, we collect the following personal information:

  • Full Name (First Name and Last Name)
  • Admission Number (Voter ID)
  • Email Address
  • Faculty and Program of Study
  • Year of Study

2.2 Biometric Data

If you choose to use fingerprint verification, we collect and store:

  • Fingerprint credential ID (not the actual fingerprint image)
  • Fingerprint enrollment timestamp
  • Fingerprint verification status

Note: We do NOT store your actual fingerprint image. Only a cryptographic credential ID is stored for verification purposes.

2.3 Voting Data

When you cast your vote, we record:

  • Your selected candidate per sector
  • Timestamp of your vote
  • Blockchain transaction hash (SHA-256)
  • IP address (for security auditing)
  • Browser and device information

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To verify your eligibility to vote
  • To ensure each student votes only once
  • To record and count votes accurately
  • To generate blockchain hashes for vote immutability
  • To send vote confirmation emails
  • To authenticate your identity during voting (including fingerprint verification)
  • To maintain audit trails for election transparency
  • To improve system security and performance

4. Blockchain Transparency

The IUEA Blockchain Voting System uses SHA-256 cryptographic hashing to secure votes. When you cast your vote, a unique transaction hash is generated and stored on our blockchain ledger. This hash is publicly verifiable without revealing your identity or your specific vote choice.

Example Hash: 0x8b5f3a2e9d1c7f4e6a2b8c3d5e7f9a1b2c3d4e5f6a7b8c9d0e1f2a3b4c5d6e7f8a9

This transparency ensures that anyone can verify that votes were counted correctly without compromising voter privacy.

5. Data Protection and Security

  • Passwords are encrypted using bcrypt hashing
  • All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
  • Fingerprint data is stored as cryptographic credentials, not actual fingerprints
  • Votes are immutable once recorded on the blockchain
  • Regular security audits are conducted
  • Access to voter data is restricted to authorized election officials only

6. Your Rights

As a voter, you have the following rights:

  • Right to access your personal information
  • Right to update your account information
  • Right to request deletion of your account (after election period)
  • Right to receive a copy of your vote receipt (transaction hash)
  • Right to verify that your vote was counted
  • Right to lodge a complaint with the IUEA Electoral Commission

7. Data Retention

Voter registration data is retained for the duration of the election period and for audit purposes as required by IUEA regulations. Blockchain transaction hashes are retained permanently for election transparency and historical reference. Fingerprint credential data is retained only for the active voter and can be removed upon account deletion.

8. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • EmailJS - For sending vote confirmation and password reset emails
  • WebAuthn API - For fingerprint verification (Windows Hello)

These services comply with data protection standards and do not store your voting data.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
